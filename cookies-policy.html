COOKIE POLICY

In order to provide a more personalized service, the Jesus-travel.com website uses cookies to collect and store information. A cookie is a small informative file, which is placed on the user's device (e.g. PC, mobile phone) and which allows them to remember their choices, dates and previous routes, that is, they store the user's preferences regarding the use of the website, personalizing your experience, so that it is not necessary to reconfigure the site each time you visit it.

Analytical cookies: these cookies gather information regarding your use of the website, for example: which pages you access most frequently. This data may be used to help optimize our web pages and make browsing easier. These cookies do not collect information that identifies you. All information gathered by these cookies is aggregated and, as such, anonymous.

Functionality cookies: these cookies allow website pages to remember the options made by the user during navigation, that is, they store the user's preferences regarding the use of the website, so that it is not necessary to reconfigure the website each time the website is used. visit.
